AuTax: A Self-Driving Taxi

AuTax is an innovative project aimed at creating a fully autonomous taxi capable of navigating complex urban environments. This project integrates cutting-edge technologies to deliver a seamless self-driving experience.

Features

  • Autonomous navigation through city streets.
  • Advanced obstacle detection and avoidance.
  • Integration with a user-friendly mobile app for booking.
  • Real-time traffic updates and route optimization.

Technologies Used

  • Computer Vision with OpenCV and YOLO for object detection.
  • ROS2 for robotic control and communication.
  • LiDAR and Ultrasonic sensors for mapping and navigation.
  • Python and C++ for core programming.

Challenges Faced

The development process involved overcoming several challenges, such as tuning the navigation algorithms for complex city environments, ensuring safety during dynamic traffic conditions, and optimizing computational resources for real-time processing.

What AuTax Provides

AuTax is designed to replace expensive platforms like TurtleBot, offering a practical and cost-effective solution for learning and practicing ROS (Robot Operating System). With AuTax, you can:

  • Start ROS practice without investing in costly hardware.
  • Access an affordable yet powerful alternative to TurtleBot.
  • Utilize state-of-the-art self-driving capabilities to explore robotics concepts.
  • Develop and test custom navigation algorithms on a flexible platform.
  • Focus on learning and innovating instead of worrying about budget constraints.

By leveraging AuTax, students, researchers, and hobbyists can dive into the world of robotics with minimal upfront costs, making it an ideal platform for anyone passionate about automation and AI.
